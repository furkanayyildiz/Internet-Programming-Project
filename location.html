2H3F

2H3F Cafe offers its guests an unrivaled environment with both its service concept and its magnificent nature.

Serving in Besyol since 1996, 2H3F Cafe continues to bring quality and comfort to its distinguished customers with its renewed garden and interior.

In the garden, a wonderful atmosphere surrounded by lush green trees welcomes you. A small pool in the middle of the garden and wooden flooring, whose floors resemble a ship deck, are complementary elements for enjoyable hours.
