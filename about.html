ABOUT

US



As 2H3F Turkey, which opened its first branch in Turkey in Florya, Istanbul in 1996, we are the leader of the Turkish food industry with a total of 530 branches in 69 provinces, primarily in Istanbul, Izmir, Ankara and Bursa. As 2H3F Turkey, we are proud to open 10,000 branches around the world in Turkey, we are the most widespread food distribution chain in Turkey as well as in the world. 2H3F currently operates more than 14,000 branches in 85 countries worldwide.


As 2H3F Turkey, we pioneered an important innovation in the sector by bringing the "home service" approach to the catering sector. We are in a leading position in the sector with the ideas we have developed and the projects we have implemented. We work with great passion to prepare the most delicious meals and offer our guests very advantageous opportunities with our campaigns. We deliver the most delicious meals in 30 minutes; We do not charge any fees for meals ordered under the "Instant Free" guarantee in case of any delay. We lead the Turkish food industry with all these privileges we offer. As 2H3F, we have achieved all of the targets we have set before us so far. As the 2H3F Turkey family, we firmly believe that we will grow with different successes in the coming years.
